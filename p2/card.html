Border Collie

Border collies have high energy and stamina with a workaholic attitude.

  • TYPE: Purebred
  • LIFESPAN: 12-15 yrs.
  • HEIGHT: 18-22 in.
  • WEIGHT: 27-45 lbs

The Border Collie is a medium sized dog breed which was bred in early Britain to help herd sheep. The breed is extremely intelligent and has a strong working drive which makes the breed easy to train and highly skilled in many agility sports.
